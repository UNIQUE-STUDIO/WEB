# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Give constructive feedback
- Accept different viewpoints
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to: **uniqe.studio@yandex.ru**

Maintainers will review and respond within 48 hours.
